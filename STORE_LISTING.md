Find your people. 💜

Chasr is the dating and social app built for the trans community. Whether you're looking for dates, friends, or just someone to chat with — Chasr connects you with people who get you.

**FEATURES:**
• Browse nearby profiles with grid and map views
• See who's online RIGHT NOW
• Match and chat with people in your area
• Live GPS location with privacy controls
• Boost your profile for more visibility
• Incognito mode for private browsing
• Global access to browse anywhere in the world

**BUILT FOR THE TRANS COMMUNITY:**
• Inclusive identity options (trans woman, trans man, non-binary, genderqueer, and more)
• Pronoun display on every profile
• Safe space with community guidelines
• Verified profiles for authenticity

**PRIVACY & SAFETY:**
• Your location is only shared when you choose
• Easy block and report tools
• Discreet mode to hide your activity
• Secure account protection

Chasr is free to download and use. Upgrade to Chasr Plus or Premium for extra features.
